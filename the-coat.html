The coat is the personal brand of a Ukrainian fashion designer preparing to launch a new collection. The project covered both a fresh logo and a redesigned online store built to carry the identity of the brand and sell the latest pieces.

My goal was to give the label a confident visual voice and an e-commerce experience that feels as considered as the clothing itself.
